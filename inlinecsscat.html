When you get lonely, adopt a Cat!

Siamese Cat

Lifespan: 15-20 years

Characteristics:

  • Distinctive color points on the ears, face, paws, and tail
  • Blue almond-shaped eyes
  • Short coat with a sleek appearance

They are the extroverts of the feline world



Maine Coon Cat

Lifespan 13-14 years.

Characteristics

  • They have a muscular, long, rectangular body with a broad chest
  • They have, round paws with tufts of hair sticking up from the toes
  • Their head is slightly longer in length than width, with high cheekbones, a distinctive square muzzle and large

Maine coons are incredibly intelligent.


Persian Cat

Lifespan 12-17 years

Characteristics of the Persian Cat

  • They have snub noses, chubby cheeks, and long hair
  • Animated, wide-set eyes
  • They are muscly, heavy-bond, medium to large-sized cat with short, thick legs and big pawssett

They shed frequently
